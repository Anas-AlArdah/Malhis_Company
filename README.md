---

## About The Project

**Malhis Wood & Tiles** is a long-established company with a strong reputation in supplying wood products, roof tiles, animal feed, carpentry services, and seasonal poultry sales.

This project aims to create the company's official digital platform, reflecting its professionalism, experience, and commitment to quality. The website is designed to provide visitors with an intuitive way to explore the company's offerings, learn about its services, and connect with the business through a modern and reliable online experience.

The objective is not simply to build a company website, but to establish a digital identity that accurately represents the scale, credibility, and legacy of the company while providing a solid foundation for future growth.

---

## Vision
